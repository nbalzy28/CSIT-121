Green Goblin

The origins story of Green Goblin

The Green Goblin's true identity is Norman Osborn, a wealthy and successful businessman who became obsessed with gaining power. Seeking an advantage over his competitors, Osborn financed a risky experiment to enhance his physical abilities and intelligence. The experiment went awry, causing Osborn to develop a split personality and adopt the persona of the Green Goblin. As the Green Goblin, Osborn wreaked havoc on New York City and targeted Spider-Man as his archenemy. Using advanced technology and a variety of weapons, the Green Goblin engaged in battles with Spider-Man, sometimes allying with other villains such as the Sinister Six. Ultimately, the Green Goblin's reign of terror would come to an end, but his legacy and influence on Spider-Man's life would continue for years to come.
