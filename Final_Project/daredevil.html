Daredevil

The origins story of Daredevil

Matt Murdock was a young boy when he was blinded by a toxic chemical accident while saving a man from being hit by a truck. The chemical not only rendered him permanently blind but also heightened his other senses to superhuman levels. Raised by his father, a boxer who taught him to never give up and to fight for what's right, Matt grew up to become a successful lawyer in Hell's Kitchen, New York. But at night, he dons a red suit and becomes Daredevil, the masked vigilante who uses his heightened senses and combat skills to protect his city from crime and corruption.
